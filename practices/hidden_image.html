What is Harry Potter?

The seven book series by J.K. Rowling is a beloved classic for many people. It tells the story of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his adventures at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Along the way, he makes friends, faces challenges, and has to confront the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. The series explores themes of friendship, bravery, and the power of love. With its memorable characters, fun plotlines, Humor, and deep themes, the Harry Potter series has been loved by readers all over the world.

About the author and how the series was written:

The Author J.K. Rowling had the idea for the story of Harry Potter one day on a delayed train in 1990. She had always loved fantasy and so came the idea of the series. She wanted to create a world where a child could escape adult authority, so the after the intial concept came an epic seven book series. She saw the books as a way to share her passion for reading with a new generation. The series was based on her own hardship of losing her mother, so the books provided an escape and a sense of power that she felt was missing from her own. Her finshed writing her first book, Harry Potter and the Philosopher's stone, in 1995. It was then published on June 26, 1997. The book was a huge hit and it was follwed by the next six. The seventh book, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, was released on July 21, 2007. From a simple idea on a train, became one of the most famous book series of all time.

He is known for having not empathy and he is responsible for countless deaths, including Harry's parents. His rise and fall shaped the entire story as he attempts to conquer the world and destroy Harry once and for all.

The Houses:


Gryffindor:

Founded by Godric Gryffindor, this house values bravery, courage, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion, and its colors are red and gold.

Hufflepuff:

Founded by Helga Hufflepuff, this house values hard work, loyalty, and fair play. Its emblematic animal is the badger, and its colors are yellow and black.

Ravenclaw:

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house values intelligence, wisdom, and creativity. Its emblematic animal is the eagle, and its colors are blue and silver.

Slytherin:

Founded by Salazar Slytherin, this house values ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness. Its emblematic animal is the serpent, and its colors are green and silver.
