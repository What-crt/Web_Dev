One of the best book series- Heroes of Olympus

The Heroes of Olympus is an sequel to the orginal Percy Jackson series. The story folows the adventure of seven demigods on their quest to save the world. It is filled with adventure, monsters, and mythology. It is an amazing series with themes such as friendship and conflict. The author, Rick Riordan, writes in a way which helps to draw you in, with humor and real emotions. If you love books that have fun characters with a mix of diffrent mytholigies and adventures then you will love The Heroes of Olympus series.
