Zion National Park

Why Zion?

Zion is a national park and is known for its tall mountians and beautiful scenery. It is one of the most popular visting spots in utah. It is great for visiting because of its stunning scenery, activities, and opportunities for adventure!

Reasons to go zion:

  • Hiking/Backpacking The Narrows(a river hike), Angels Landing(permit required for the chained sections), Scout Lookout, Emerald Pools, Canyon Overlook, Watchman trail, West tim and East Rim Trail, and La Verkin Creek Trail
  • Canyoneering
  • Bike Trails
  • Horseback Riding
  • Camping
  • Waterfalls
  • Scenic Drive
