Zion National Park

Why Zion?

Zion is a national park and is known for its tall mountians and beautiful scenery. It is one of the most popular visting spots in utah. It is great for visiting because of its stunning scenery, activities, and opportunities for adventure!

What can you do?

  • Hiking/Backpacking -The Narrows, Angels Landing, Scout Lookout, Canyon Overlook, Watchman trail, and La Verkin Creek Trail
  • Canyoneering -Pine Creek, keyhole Canyon, Ladder Canyon, and Imlay Canyon.
  • Horseback Riding -Hop Valley Trail, Sawmill rim Trail, Telephone Canyon Trail, and Cable Mountain trail.
  • Camping -Zion Canyon Campgroud and RV Resort, Watchman Campground, Lava Point Campground.
  • Waterfalls -Emerald Pools, Weeping Rock, Temple of Sinawava, and Archangel Falls.
