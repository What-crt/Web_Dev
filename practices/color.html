Analogous

Analogous colors are three or more colors right next to each other on the color wheel.

(EXAMPLE: purple, blue and blue-green)

Complementary

Complementary colors are colors pairs of colors that are directky opposite each other on the color wheel.

(EXAMPLE: blue and orange)

Triadic

Triadic colors are three colors that are equally spaced on the color wheel, forming a triangle and creating a vibrant and balanced color scheme.

(EXAMPLE: green, purple, and orange)
