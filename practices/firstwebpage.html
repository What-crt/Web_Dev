-FORREST FRANK
Member: Forrest Frank

-JOSIAH QUEEN
Member: Josiah Queen
started in 2020 in New Port Richey, Florida


-THE OKEE DOKEE BROTHERS
Members: Joe Mailander and Justin Lansing
Started in 2005 in Minneapolis, Minnesota


-RAYNES
Members: Mark Race, Mat Charley, and Joe Berger
Started in 2017 in Los Angles, California


-IMAGINE DRAGONS
Members: Dan Reynolds, Wayne Sermon, Ben McKee, and Daniel Platzman
Started in 2008 in Las Vegas, Nevada


-OWL CITY
Member: Adam Young
Started in 2007 in Owatonna, Minnesota

List of most popular songs from each band and one fun fact:

-TWENTY ONE PILOTS
Popular songs: Heathens, Stressed Out, and Ride
Fun Fact:Twenty One Pilots got their name from the Arthur Miller play "All My Sons," which is about a man who knowingly sends out faulty airplane parts, leading to the deaths of 21 pilots.
Wikipedia page to Twenty one pilots

-FORREST FRANK
Popular songs: UP, GOOD DAY, and GOD IS GOOD
Fun Fact: Forrest Frank not only has his own band but also is a member of the band Surfaces with Colin Padalecki.
Wikipedia page to Forest Frank

-JOSIAH QUEEN
Popular songs: The Prodigal, Dusty Bibles, and I am Barabbas
Fun Fact: Before his music career took off, he worked as a church janitor and car wash attendant. He began writing songs at age nine, was a nominee for New Artist of the Year at the 2024 GMA Dove Awards, and his debut album, The Prodigal, reached #1 on the Billboard Top Christian Albums chart.
Josiah Queen website

-THE OKEE DOKEE BROTHERS
Popular songs: Can You Canoe?, Hope Machine, and Roll On River
Fun Fact: The Okee Dokee Brothers won a Grammy Award for Best Children's Album in 2013 for their album "Can You Canoe?"
Wikipedia page to Okee Dokee brothers

-RAYNES
Popular songs: Cast the Bronse, Lemon Drop, and Marigolds
Fun Fact: Mark Race got a tattoo of his flight number to the U.S. to commemorate the moment he joined the band, making the transatlantic connection a permanent part of their story.
Wikipedia page to Raynes

-IMAGINE DRAGONS
Popular songs: Believer, Bones, and Enemy
Fun Fact: The band's name, "Imagine Dragons," is an anagram, though the original phrase has never been officially revealed.
Wikipedia page to Imagine Dragons

-OWL CITY
Popular songs: Fireflies, Vanilla Twilight, and Good Time
Fun Fact: Adam Young created Owl City in 2007 in his Owatonna, Minnesota basement to deal with insomnia caused by his job as a Coca-Cola truck loader.
Wikipedia page to Owl City
